macOS

macOS is the current unix-based operating system by Apple inc. It is second in wide use behind Windows computers. A OS writte in c, c++, Objective-C and swift. The initial release of the macOS was in 2012. It is based on technologies that were developed at NeXT between 1985 and 1997. Since 2002 the macOS has been preinstalled on apple computers.

Part of the apple OS's, the is the iOS, the operating system that runs on ther apple smart phones. The iOS has been around since 2007, when apple announced the release of their first iPhone. iOS is the second widely used OS for smart phones after android.
